Brand Protection

At Titan, we take immense pride in crafting the best-in-class watches that are designed to stand the test of time. As we strive to ensure a trustworthy shopping experience for our customers, it is important to note that sale of counterfeit products is illegal. However, we've noticed that there are counterfeit products being sold in the market, and we take this matter very seriously.


Counterfeit products are fake copies of the original products that are designed to deceive customers into believing that they are purchasing genuine products. Unfortunately, these counterfeit products are usually of inferior quality and can cause problems to customers.


Counterfeiters often use low-quality materials and substandard manufacturing processes, which can result in watches that may break or malfunction easily. These watches may also contain harmful chemicals or materials, which can cause skin irritation, allergies, or other health problems. In addition, counterfeit watches don't come with after-sales support and warranty that authentic Titan watches offer. So, if a customer encounters any issues with their counterfeit watch, they won't be able to get any support or repair services from Titan.
